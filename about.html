About Our Search Engine

Built for simplicity, speed, and smarter searching.

The Keyword-Based Search Engine for Text Documents is designed to provide a fast, reliable, and efficient method for retrieving relevant information from a variety of documents. Whether you are a student, researcher, or professional, this tool helps you locate the information you need quickly, so you can focus on what matters most.

Key Features

Efficient Document Indexing: Fast and accurate search results from indexed documents.
Keyword Search with Spell Checking: Real-time spelling suggestions and keyword matching to ensure accurate results.
User-Friendly Interface: Simple, clean design for ease of use across various devices.
Secure Accounts: Safe document upload and management with user authentication.

Our Team

Our goal is to build tools that are not only functional but also intuitive and user-friendly. Through this project, we aim to make document searching more efficient, saving users time and effort.
